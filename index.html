Bella Goth

Queen of the Sims Franchise

Bella Goth has been a constant presence in the Sims game universe.

Bella Goth ( /ˈbɛlə ɡɒθ/) (née Bachelor) is one of the pre-made Sims shipped with The Sims game, in which she lives with her husband, Mortimer Goth, and her daughter, Cassandra Goth. Between The Sims and The Sims 2, Bella and Mortimer had one more child, Alexander Goth. She also had an elder brother named Michael Bachelor.
